# Water Ring — Support & FAQ

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: How do I play Water Ring?**
A: Tap and hold the screen to create water currents that guide rings onto matching pegs. Release to let the rings settle. Land all rings before time runs out to complete each challenge.

**Q: Is Water Ring free to play?**
A: Yes! Water Ring is free to download and play. Optional in-app purchases are available for cosmetics and an ad-free experience.

**Q: Does Water Ring require an internet connection?**
A: An internet connection is needed for leaderboards, daily challenges, and cloud saves. Core gameplay works offline.

### Account & Progress

**Q: How do I save my progress?**
A: Your progress is automatically saved to the cloud when signed in. You can also play as a guest with local-only saves.

**Q: I lost my progress. How do I recover it?**
A: If you were signed in, reinstall the app and sign in with the same account. Your progress will sync automatically. Guest progress cannot be recovered after uninstalling.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data and cannot be undone.

### In-App Purchases

**Q: How do I restore my purchases?**
A: Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase.

**Q: I was charged but didn't receive my purchase.**
A: Try restoring purchases first. If that doesn't work, contact us at support@syntaxandco.com with your purchase receipt.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The game is running slowly or crashing.**
A: Try closing other apps, restarting your device, or reinstalling the game. Ensure your device meets the minimum requirements (iOS 15+ / Android 8+).

**Q: Sound or haptics are not working.**
A: Check Settings > Sound & Haptics within the game. Also verify your device is not in silent mode and haptics are enabled in your device settings.
