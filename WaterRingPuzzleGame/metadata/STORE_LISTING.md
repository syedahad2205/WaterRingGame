**HOW TO PLAY**
Tap and hold to create water currents that guide colorful rings onto matching pegs. Time your moves carefully — each ring must land perfectly before time runs out!

- Realistic water physics powered by a custom fluid simulation engine
- 15 stunning visual themes — from Ocean depths to Galaxy vistas
- Daily challenges with global leaderboards
- Achievement system with 20 unlockable milestones
- Daily missions and weekly challenges
- Beautiful glassmorphism UI with smooth animations
- Haptic feedback that makes every ring landing feel satisfying
- Cloud save — pick up where you left off on any device
