# Privacy Policy — Water Ring

**Last updated:** [DATE]
**Effective date:** [DATE]

## Overview
Water Ring ("the Game") is developed by Syntax & Co. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- Display name (optional)
- Game progress and scores

### Information Collected Automatically
- Device type and operating system
- Game analytics (scores, play sessions, feature usage)
- Crash reports and performance data
- Advertising identifiers (for personalized ads, with consent)

### Information from Third Parties
- Firebase Authentication (anonymous ID)
- RevenueCat (purchase history, anonymized)
- Google AdMob (ad interaction data)

## How We Use Information
- To provide and improve the Game
- To track your progress and display leaderboards
- To personalize your experience
- To display relevant advertisements
- To detect and prevent fraud
- To analyze usage patterns and improve gameplay

## Data Storage and Security
- Game data is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud)
- Local data is stored on your device using encrypted storage
- We use industry-standard security measures
- We do not sell your personal information

## Children's Privacy
- The Game does not knowingly collect information from children under 13
- If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us

## Your Rights
- Access your data
- Delete your account and data
- Opt out of personalized advertising
- Export your data

## Advertising
- We display ads through Google AdMob
- You can opt out of personalized ads in Settings
- Ad-free experience available via in-app purchase

## Changes
We may update this policy. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
