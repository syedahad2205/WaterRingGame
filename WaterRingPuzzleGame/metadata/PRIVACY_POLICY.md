# Privacy Policy — Water Ring Puzzle Game

**Last Updated:** 2024-01-01
**Effective Date:** 2024-01-01

---

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Water Ring ("we", "our", or "the game") collects, uses, and shares information when you play our mobile game. By playing Water Ring, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Username and display name** — chosen by you during onboarding
- **Avatar / profile image** — uploaded optionally
- **Country** — selected optionally for leaderboard filtering

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Gameplay data** — challenge numbers completed, scores, star ratings, continue usage, XP earned
- **Device information** — device model, OS version, screen resolution, language settings
- **Performance data** — frame rate, memory usage, crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics)
- **Analytics events** — screen views, button taps, feature interactions (via Firebase Analytics)
- **Purchase transactions** — IAP product IDs and purchase timestamps (not payment card data — processed by Apple/Google)
- **IP address** — used transiently for geolocation; not stored beyond the session

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- Payment card numbers, bank account information
- Government-issued ID or social security numbers
- Precise GPS location
- Contacts, photos, or files from your device
- Health data

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

| Purpose | Legal Basis |
|---------|-------------|
| Operate the game, sync progress across devices | Contract performance |
| Display leaderboards with username and score | Legitimate interest |
| Detect and prevent cheating / anti-cheat | Legitimate interest |
| Send push notifications about daily challenges | Consent (opt-in) |
| Improve gameplay balance via aggregate analytics | Legitimate interest |
| Comply with legal obligations | Legal obligation |
| Process in-app purchases (handled by Apple/Google) | Contract performance |

We do **not** sell your personal information to third parties.

---

## 4. Information Sharing

We share data with:

- **Firebase (Google LLC)** — authentication, database, analytics, crash reporting, cloud functions. Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- **Apple / Google** — in-app purchase processing. We receive only transaction confirmations, not payment details.
- **Law enforcement** — only when required by valid legal process

We do not share data with advertising networks, data brokers, or marketing platforms.

---

## 5. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Player profile and progress | Until account deletion request |
| Gameplay events (analytics) | 14 months (Firebase default) |
| Crash reports | 90 days |
| Purchase receipts | 7 years (legal requirement) |
| Inactive accounts | Deleted after 24 months of inactivity |

---

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- **Access** — request a copy of data we hold about you
- **Correction** — update incorrect information via the in-app Profile screen
- **Deletion** — request deletion of your account and associated data via Settings → Delete Account
- **Portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Opt-out** — disable analytics collection in Settings → Privacy

To exercise any right, email: **privacy@waterringgame.com**
We will respond within 30 days.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Water Ring is rated 4+ (iOS) and Everyone (Android). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us personal information, contact privacy@waterringgame.com and we will delete it promptly.

For users in the EU/UK under 16, we rely on parental consent or apply additional restrictions as required by GDPR and COPPA.

---

## 8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including:
- TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest in Firebase
- HMAC-SHA256 coin ledger integrity checks to prevent tampering
- Firebase Security Rules restricting data access to authenticated owners only
- Firestore rules reviewed and signed off by two engineers before deployment

---

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States and other countries where Google operates Firebase infrastructure. By using the game, you consent to these transfers. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses for transfers from the EEA/UK.

---

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes via an in-app notification at least 7 days before changes take effect. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance.
